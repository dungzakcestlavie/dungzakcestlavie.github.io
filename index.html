Artist Bio

DUNGZAK CESTLAVIE is a Korean contemporary artist exploring the origins of light, memory, and being. His practice traces fleeting emotions, the birth of light through wounds, and the cycles of disappearance and rebirth.

Based in Seoul and working across Berlin, Paris, Amsterdam, Warsaw, and Moscow, he integrates painting, text, sound, video, and archival forms to pursue an ethics of light and dignity of memory. Light in his work operates not as effect but as a mental architecture awakening emotional restoration and humaneness.

His paintings are prayers, documents, and gazes. Through traces and intervals, he reconstructs the foundations of feeling and expands the DCAR Aesthetic Reports and the DUNGZAK ART UNIVERSE.

This world is a quiet declaration: warmth that remains after pain, memory that refuses to fade, and the enduring dignity of the human.
